About Practica11y

What is Practica11y?

Practica11y is a gamified, browser-based learning platform for web accessibility. It helps developers build practical skills through interactive, hands-on challenges — right in the browser, no backend required.

The mission is to raise awareness for accessibility, sharpen understanding of WCAG guidelines, and make inclusive development a natural habit. Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your expertise, Practica11y meets you where you are.
